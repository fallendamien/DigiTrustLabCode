Pro blog rules — build these into Bricks
1
Max content width 720px SEO
65–70 chars per line = optimal Malay readability. Set on main column container.
2
Featured image — 1200×630px ratio Must
Every post needs one. This is what shows on Facebook share + Google snippets.
3
Sticky sidebar on desktop Must
MailerLite opt-in stays visible as reader scrolls. This is your email list engine.
4
Orange CTA box inside every post body Must
1 contextual product/affiliate box per post. Not banner — embedded mid-content.
5
Reading time in post meta SEO
Reduces bounce rate. Rank Math calculates this automatically.
6
WhatsApp in share buttons Must
Malaysian audience shares via WhatsApp, not X/Twitter. Prioritise WA in share row.
7
Author bio with real name + photo SEO
Google E-E-A-T signal. Replace "admin" with Zamri Rosli + short 1-line bio.
8
No sidebar on mobile — single column Must
99% of your audience is mobile. Sidebar collapses below post content on small screens.
