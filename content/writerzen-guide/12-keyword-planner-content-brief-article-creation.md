# 3.3 Content Brief and Article Creation in Keyword Planner

## Overview

This lesson covers how to turn keyword cluster insights into actionable content — from generating a content brief to creating full articles using WriterZen's AI assistant.

Each keyword cluster represents a potential article. The Keyword Planner streamlines the entire process from cluster to published content.

## Suggest Content Brief

Previously, planning content from a keyword cluster meant manually researching each keyword to identify search intent and match your content tone accordingly. WriterZen removes this step with the **Suggest Content Brief** button.

One click takes all the keyword insights and metrics from a cluster and generates a complete article template — covering every angle to make it as impactful as possible.

**Content brief components:**

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Content Angle** | Based on target audience and author's perspective — goal is to engage the audience and address their pain points |
| **Target Audience** | The predefined market or segment intended for the content — helps tailor tone and messaging |
| **Author's Perspective** | The recommended viewpoint, beliefs, and experiences the author brings — influences tone, language, and overall message |
| **Seed Keyword** | Pre-set to the highest volume keyword in the cluster |
| **Content Format** | Suggested structure — options include Blog Post (default), Review, Listicle, How-to Guide |
| **Writing Tone** | Sets the overall mood — options include Formal (default), Informative, Explanatory, Friendly, Conversational, Analytical, Critical |

> **Tips:**
> - Click the Suggest Content Brief button again to regenerate a new brief (overwrites the previous one)
> - Manually adjust any field to match your specific situation
> - All changes are saved automatically
> - The content brief guides both your writers and WriterZen's AI — it is the input that shapes AI output quality

## Creating a New Article

Once your content brief is ready, click **Create Article**. A pop-up appears with three tabs:

### General Tab
- Create a new project or save to an existing one
- Activate AI assistant and/or Google NLP
- Choose AI generation mode:
  - **Write article title, description, and outline** — generates the structural skeleton
  - **Write the whole article** — generates a complete content piece
- Click **Advanced Settings** to set priority and deadline

### Content Brief Tab
- Displays the content brief preview
- Review and adjust before generating

### Shared Access Tab
- Add writers by their WriterZen-registered email address
- Set guest permissions:
  - View competitor panel
  - View related questions
  - Check plagiarism

Once setup is complete, go directly to the article or continue planning other clusters.

## Bulk Content Generation

You can scale up content production by multi-selecting clusters and generating briefs and articles in bulk:

1. Select a **topic** — this automatically selects all clusters within it
2. Deselect any specific clusters you want to skip
3. Click **Suggest Content Brief** — WriterZen generates briefs for all selected clusters simultaneously
4. Review each brief by toggling between clusters and make any necessary adjustments
5. Click **Create Article** → create a new project, set AI mode, configure priority and shared access
6. Click **Create** — AI generates all articles for selected clusters at once

The result: a full project with multiple articles generated in seconds.

## Tips for Getting the Best AI Output

- **Better input = better output** — follow the AI-suggested content brief first, then modify to suit your preferences
- **Treat AI as your assistant, not your leader** — AI is a tool to enhance your creative process, not replace your expertise and judgment
- Always revise AI-generated content and add your own human touch before publishing

## Key Takeaway

The Keyword Planner → Content Brief → Create Article workflow automates the most time-consuming parts of content planning. What used to take hours of manual research per article can now be done in seconds — at scale — without sacrificing content quality when used correctly.
