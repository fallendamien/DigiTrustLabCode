# 6.2 Team Function: Collaboration in Content Creator

## Overview

This lesson covers how to collaborate inside the Content Creator tool. The three key benefits of the Team Function are:

1. Invite members and guests to work on the same platform
2. Delegate and track projects
3. Supported team communication

---

## Benefit 1: Invite Members and Guests

### Members vs. Guests

| | Member | Guest |
|---|---|---|
| **Definition** | Part of your paid team | External person (e.g. freelancer) |
| **Seat required** | ✅ Yes — you pay per seat | ❌ No seat required |
| **Workspace access** | Full — view/edit/delete all projects and articles | None — only accesses articles shared with them |
| **WriterZen account required** | ✅ Yes | ✅ Yes |

### How to Share Articles

**Method 1 — Via Article Settings:**
1. Click the edit icon on the project
2. Open the **Shared Access** tab in the article settings pop-up
3. Enter the writer's email address
4. Optionally click the **link icon** to copy and share the direct link

**Method 2 — Via Share Button:**
1. Open the article
2. Click the **Share** button
3. Enter the email address
4. Optionally copy the direct link

Both members and guests are notified via email and in-app notification when an article is shared with them.

> Anyone without access who tries to open a shared link is redirected to a request access page — the task owner is then notified to approve or decline.

### Guest Permissions

By default, guests can only edit the article content. You can extend their access in the Shared Access pop-up under **Guest Permissions**:

- ✅ Plagiarism Checker
- ✅ Competitors panel
- ✅ Related Questions

> ⚠️ Guests cannot view, edit, or delete any other content in your workspace. This protects your workspace when working with untrusted freelancers.

## Benefit 2: Delegate and Track Projects

### Project Listing View

The Content Creator dashboard shows all projects with:
- Current progress status per project
- Human resources — task owner through to assigned writers
- **Process column** — colour-coded progress indicator:
  - 🟢 Green = completed
  - 🟠 Orange = one or more tasks still pending
- Use the **search/filter** on the process column to quickly identify projects needing attention

### Inside Each Project

Click any project to see:

| Tab | What It Shows |
|---|---|
| **Articles** | Total articles in the project + individual article progress |
| **Project Members** | All members and guests added to the project, their permissions, articles owned, and articles written |

**Article listing columns:**

| Column | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Status** | Current state of each article (Writing / Reviewing / Approved) |
| **Deadline** | Set by the task owner to track timeline |
| **Task Owner** | Who owns and is responsible for the article |
| **Writer** | Who is writing the article |

### Managing Task Ownership

Any member who creates a task automatically becomes that task's owner. To change ownership:
1. Click the **Share icon** inside the document
2. Open the article settings pop-up
3. Click **Replace** on the Task Owner field

### Removing Member Roles from a Project
Click the **delete icon** next to any member in the Project Members tab to revoke their role on that project.

## Benefit 3: Supported Team Communication

### Article Status Workflow

The system updates automatically — no manual input required. Every team member always sees the current status of any task in real time.

**Article status progression:**

| Status | Trigger |
|---|---|
| **Writing** | Article is being worked on |
| **Reviewing** | Writer submits the article for review |
| **Requires Edit** | Owner/editor sends it back for revision |
| **Approved** | Owner/editor approves the final article |

Any team member can update article status — enabling full delegation within your team.

### Example Team Structures

| Team Size | Suggested Structure |
|---|---|
| **Small** | Owner handles everything directly |
| **Medium** | Assign editors to review writers — owner only deals with editors |
| **Large** | Editors manage writers, a lead manages editors, owner focuses on topic ideation only |

> The more you delegate status management, the more time you free up to focus on strategy and growth.

---

## Key Takeaway

WriterZen's Team Function lets you scale your content operation without adding management overhead. Members, guests, status automation, and project tracking work together to keep everyone aligned — without constant check-ins or manual status updates.
