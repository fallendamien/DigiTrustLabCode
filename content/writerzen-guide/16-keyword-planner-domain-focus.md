# 3.7 Domain Focus in Keyword Planner

## Overview

Domain Focus (DF) is the latest update to Keyword Planner. It allows you to zoom in on a single domain and assess its performance across all keywords and clusters in your keyword list — enabling deep competitive analysis and content gap identification.

## Key Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Captured Keyword** | A keyword for which a domain has secured a ranking within the top 10 Google SERPs with at least one URL |
| **Captured Search Volume** | The cumulative search volume of all captured keywords for that domain |

## How to Activate

1. Click the **Domain Focus** button in the bottom taskbar
2. Select **Domain Focus** from the popup menu
3. Enter the specific domain you want to analyse

The Keyword Planner layout updates to a new view with detailed performance metrics for each topic and cluster.

---

## Reading the Domain Focus View

### Topic Cards

Each topic card shows two metrics for the focused domain:

| Metric | Description |
|---|---|
| **Progress bar** | Proportion of the topic's total search volume the domain has captured |
| **Weighted Rank** (blue number) | Weighted rank of the domain's captured keywords in this topic. A dash (—) means the domain has no top 10 rankings in this topic |

**Example:** verywell fit.com in the "Local Meal Plan" topic → ~80% of total search volume captured (5,010 searches), weighted rank 5.8

### Cluster Cards

Each cluster card shows:

| Metric | Description |
|---|---|
| **Progress bar** | Proportion of cluster search volume the domain has captured |
| **Weighted Rank** (blue number) | Weighted rank within the cluster. Dash = no top 10 rankings |
| **Captured / Total Keywords** | e.g. 28/55 keywords captured in this cluster |

### Keyword Data Table

For each keyword in the filtered view:
- Total search volume captured
- Average CPC
- Domain's **highest position** for that keyword
- Click any keyword to expand and see the SERP overview — the focus domain's row is highlighted

### Topic Sorting Options (Domain Focus Exclusive)

Three new sorting options appear when Domain Focus is active:
- **By Captured Volume** — highest search volume captured first
- **By Captured Keywords** — most keywords captured first
- **By Weighted Rank** — best average ranking first

## Strategy Framework: Supply & Demand Analysis

| Side | What It Represents |
|---|---|
| **Demand** | Your targeted keyword list |
| **Supply** | All domains ranking for those keywords (top sites, competitors, social media) |

Domain Focus tells you exactly how your chosen domain is performing on the supply side — cluster by cluster.

---

## Use Case 1: Analyse Your Own Domain Performance

Find content gaps and quick wins within your existing keyword list.

1. Activate Domain Focus on your own domain
2. Apply **Weak Spot filter** (minimum 1) + **Search Volume filter** (minimum 500)
3. Sort topics by volume (high to low)
4. Identify topics and clusters where your domain is capturing few keywords despite decent search volume
5. For each cluster:
   - If capturing 4/6 keywords → add the 2 missing keywords into existing ranking content, or create new content and link from the ranking article
   - If capturing 0 keywords → create new content targeting the cluster seed keyword

## Use Case 2: Combined Domain Focus + Domain Filter + DA Score

The most powerful combination for full competitive analysis.

**Step 1 — Filter by competitor performance:**
1. Open Domain Filter → Filter Rules
2. Add top 3 competitors → **Include Clusters** → **Ranked Top 10** → **Any Keywords**
3. Apply → now you see only clusters where your top 3 competitors rank

**Step 2 — Focus on your own domain within those clusters:**
1. Activate Domain Focus on your own domain
2. Analyse cluster cards to see how you perform within each competitor-dominated cluster

**Step 3 — Interpret and act:**

| Scenario | What It Means | Action |
|---|---|---|
| **Low captured volume + Low captured keywords** | Domain is not competing in this cluster | Create or optimise content — keywords in the same cluster rank together easily |
| **High captured volume + Low captured keywords** | Already capturing the highest-volume keywords — lower priority for remaining ones | Focus on improving weighted rank to increase traffic from existing rankings |
| **High captured volume + High captured keywords + Low weighted rank** | Domain ranks for most keywords but positions are weak — quality issue | Improve content quality to push rankings higher and increase actual traffic |

## Summary: What Domain Focus Enables

| Goal | How |
|---|---|
| Identify your own content gaps | Domain Focus on your domain + weak spot/volume filters |
| Benchmark against competitors | Domain Focus on competitor domain + same filters |
| Find clusters to prioritise | Sort by captured volume or weighted rank |
| Analyse weak rankings | High capture + low weighted rank = content quality issue |
| Find quick wins | Low capture + weak spots present = create content now |

## Key Takeaway

Domain Focus is the most granular competitive intelligence feature in WriterZen. Combine it with the Domain Filter and DA Score to get a complete picture of the market — where you stand, where your competitors are strong, and exactly which clusters represent your fastest path to capturing more search volume.
