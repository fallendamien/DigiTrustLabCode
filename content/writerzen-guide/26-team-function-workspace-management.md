# 6.1 Team Function: Workspace Management

## Overview

The WriterZen Workspace is your personal work area from which you can invite and manage team members, as well as join other workspaces. It is designed to make collaboration with multiple writers and editors straightforward.

> **Note:** You can be a member of multiple workspaces and switch between them with a single click from your dashboard.

## Three Main Areas

| Area | Access |
|---|---|
| **Seats & Members** | All members can view |
| **Limits & Usage** | All members can view |
| **Billing** | Workspace owner only |

---

## Seats & Members

Displays all members of your workspace team, including:
- Total seats in use (one seat per member including your own)
- Available seats
- Removed seats
- Total members
- Pending invites
- Each member's email address and latest activity timestamp
- **Search filter** — quickly find a specific team member as your team grows

### Permissions (Current Version)
- **Workspace owner only** — can modify account permissions, team settings, and billing
- **All team members** — can view and edit all content inside the workspace, use all tools, create their own tasks, and view/edit/delete all other tasks

---

## Limits & Usage

Shows your workspace's tool credit limits and up-to-date usage across all members.

- All limits apply to the entire workspace — shared across all members
- Each time any member uses a tool (Content Creator, Topic Discovery, Keyword Explorer, etc.), the relevant credit is deducted from the workspace's available balance
- Visit **freshdesk.writerzen.com** for detailed information on plan limits

---

## Managing Seats

WriterZen manages team members by **seat** — one seat per member.

### Buy More Seats
1. Go to **Seats & Members**
2. Click **Buy Extra Seats**
3. Enter the number of seats required

### Add a Team Member
1. Ensure you have available seats
2. Click on an available seat
3. Enter the member's WriterZen email address
4. Click **Invite**
5. Member receives an email invitation — once accepted, they are added to the workspace
6. To resend a missed invitation: click the **three dots** next to the member → **Resend Invitation**

### Remove a Team Member
1. Click the **three dots** next to the member
2. Select **Remove Member**

> Only the workspace owner can remove members.

### Remove Seats
Seats must be empty (member removed) before you can remove the seat itself.

1. Click the **three dots** next to the seat
2. Select **Remove Seat**

- Seat removal is reflected on your **next billing cycle**
- You can **reactivate** a removed seat before the expiration date if needed
- ⚠️ Unassigned seats are subject to automatic deletion

---

## Leaving a Workspace

| Situation | How to Leave |
|---|---|
| Logged into **your own** workspace, leaving **another** workspace | Click your workspace link (top right) → three dots on the other workspace → **Leave this workspace** |
| Logged into the **other** workspace | Go to Seats & Members → click **Leave this workspace** |

> You **cannot** leave your own workspace.
> When you leave a workspace, all tasks you created are transferred to the workspace owner.
