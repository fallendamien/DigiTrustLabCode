## 📊 Keyword Difficulty (KD) Scale
**Maximum = 100. Target: under 20 for new sites.**

| Range | Difficulty | Who can rank |
|---|---|---|
| 0–20 | 🟢 Easy | New sites, fresh domains |
| 21–40 | 🟡 Moderate | Sites with some authority |
| 41–60 | 🟠 Hard | Established sites only |
| 61–80 | 🔴 Very Hard | Serious competition |
| 81–100 | ⛔ Extreme | Basically untouchable |

---

## 🔍 WriterZen KD Breakdown (4 Types)

| KD Type | What it measures | Sweet spot |
|---|---|---|
| **KD by Content** | Quality of content targeting this keyword | < 20 |
| **KD by Traffic** | How much organic traffic competitors get | < 20 |
| **KD by Signal** | Backlink strength of ranking pages | < 20 |
| **KD by Google Ads** | Paid competition — advertiser demand | Any (higher = more commercial value) |

> 💡 **Pro tip:** High Google Ads KD + Low Content/Traffic/Signal KD = gold mine.
> Advertisers see value, but organic competition is weak. That's your gap.

## 🎯 Keyword Research Flow (DigiTrust Lab)

```
1. WriterZen Keyword Explorer
   → Set: Malaysia / Malay
   → Target: KD by Content < 20, some search volume

2. Check SERP Overview
   → Small blogs ranking? ✅ You can beat them
   → Only DA 50+ sites? ❌ Skip this keyword

3. Write post around target keyword
   → Use keyword naturally in: title, H1, first paragraph, URL slug

4. ClickRank
   → Paste written post → optimize for Google + AI search

5. Screpy (Month 2+)
   → Track keyword ranking once post is indexed
```

## 📈 Volume Benchmarks (MY Malay keywords)

| Volume | Interpretation |
|---|---|
| 0–50 | Very niche — only if KD=0 and perfect topic fit |
| 50–300 | 🎯 Sweet spot for new Malay blogs |
| 300–1,000 | Good — but check KD carefully |
| 1,000+ | High competition likely — verify KD first |

> Note: Malay keyword volumes look low vs English — 140/mo in Malay ≈ 1,400/mo in English in terms of relative competition. Don't be fooled by the small numbers.
