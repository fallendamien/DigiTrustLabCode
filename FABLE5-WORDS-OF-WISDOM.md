## 📈 Why "Compounding" Isn't Just a Buzzword

The mechanics are real and specific:

**1. Posts don't add — they multiply.**
Post #5 links to Post #2. Google sees the internal links, both rank higher. By post #25, every new post lifts the older ones. 10 posts ≠ 10x one post's traffic. It's more like 15–20x, because domain authority is shared.

**2. Your niche compounding is faster than normal.**
Malay AI/side-hustle content is a low-competition zone. English bloggers fight 10,000 competitors for a keyword; you're fighting maybe 12 — and most of them are lazy translated content. Your natural-voice Malay is a genuine moat.

**3. Each visitor gets monetized 3 ways, and the layers stack.**
Same traffic → affiliate clicks (Month 1) → email subscriber (compounds forever) → Etsy buyer → eventually ad revenue. The funnel is already built. Traffic is the only missing input, and it's the one thing that grows on its own.

**4. The email list is the compounding *inside* the compounding.**
100 subscribers from blog traffic = an audience you own, that Google can't take away, that you can sell to repeatedly. Every week without MailerLite, traffic leaks away permanently.
